About

pirotech - is the result of cooperation of Russian engineers and top-ranked German specialists. Thanks to longstanding and strong partnership, the innovative and unparalleled technology for immediate ignition suppression is now available to anyone.

Scopes of application:

Facilities of oil-and-gas-field operation, automobiles and electric vehicles, railway, marine and inland water transport, aircraft engineering, armored vehicles, residential houses and public facilities, social objects, storages and industrial spaces, power cables and cable ducts, power plants and power generation stations, server rooms. Radio and television equipment, electric switchboards and control panels, archives, museums and libraries.

Technology

Due to physical and chemical properties and use of the most advanced materials for manufacturing processes, pirotech products are highly reliable and efficient, fully autonomous, non-toxic and long-lasting.

When overheated, or exposed to sparks or flames, pirotech automatically releases fire extinguishing agent towards a source of ignition which immediately suppresses fire!

Technology Features

The basis of pirotech products is polymer matrix filled with microcapsules to contain high-performance extinguishing agent NOVEC 1230. When overheated or exposed to fire, microcapsules break down and release extinguishing agent towards a source of ignition to immediately suppress fire at initial stages. For manufacture of pirotech products, only innovative technologies and high-quality materials are used which guarantees their trouble-free operation within 5 years and safety for humans, animals and environment.

pirotech - is a new level of fire safety arrangements. A nanotechnology, based upon microencapsulation of fire extinguishing agents, significantly reduces fire risk at very initial ignition stages.

pirotech - is a standalone system to be an inertia-free and automatic element of any fire-fighting system to suppress any source of ignition. It is activated independently of warning and public address systems, automatic fire-fighting systems, emergency power cutoff and human factor.

pirotech - is a perfect addition to regular fire suppression systems to significantly improve fire protection reliability.

Products

pirotech paint

Microcapsules are used for manufacture of high-performance, fire-extinguishing paints to protect surfaces of various types from ignitions at industrial facilities (to also include nuclear power-generating stations), in railway, water and air transport, subway,housing and community amenities and public buildings: electric equipment (cable ways and ducts, distribution boards), electronic and fuel-and-power equipment. Fire-proof coatings of industrial fabrics. Fire protection of containers for transportation of high-value and hazardous cargo.

pirotech cord

pirotech cord - is an autonomous fire suppression solution, produced in the form of extinguishing cord for the protection inside of distribution boards, electric cabinets, control panels, vault areas etc. (max. vol. 1000 L).

Easy installation and flexibility of pirotech cord allows to install it as close as possible to sources of potential ignition (contact blocks, harnesses and bunches of wires and cables, electrical facilities, etc.) and prevent operation failures.

Operating temperatures: -40ºC - +80ºC
Warranty period: 5 years (no damage to equipment)
